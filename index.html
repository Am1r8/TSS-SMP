TSS SMP

TSS SMP is the minecraft server that you need. You can have lots of fun with your friends. You can experience minigames like soccer, basketball, and many more. There are stores and other places that you can visit in here. The map is inspired by Thornhill. You can sell, buy, and trade with other players in the server. There is literally no limitation to the things that you can do in this city.

 

The Experience

The Thornhill Secondary School SMP or TSS SMP, is a survival Minecraft server that includes your favorite locations in Thornhill, including TSS itself! The SMP also has a story that is [mostly an excuse for our laziness], and you can learn more about it once you join the server. You can battle your friends, buy houses, go for a ride with your car and even get a coffee from Tim Hortons. You can earn money by selling your items, and you can also earn money by killing bounties, playing mini games and selling items.
